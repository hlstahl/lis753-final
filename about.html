Hey there! I’m Heather, the gal behind Red Kitty Designs, a handmade craft and design studio in Southwest Florida. Ever since I was a little girl, I’ve loved creating and trying new things. Most of my summers growing up were spent with my grandparents in Ohio, and during that time, both of my grandmothers passed on to me their love for arts and crafts. I’ve dabbled in a little bit of everything over the years, but these days I mostly stick to fluid-acrylic painting and sewing.

When I’m not working on a creative project, you can usually find me tutoring writing at the local college, studying for my MLIS, or playing Stardew Valley.
